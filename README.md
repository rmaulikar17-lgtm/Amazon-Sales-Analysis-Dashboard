# Amazon-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
This Amazon Product Sales Analysis Dashboard is an advanced, interactive Power BI solution designed to help businesses, analysts, and decision-makers track, evaluate, and optimize product performance across Amazon’s marketplace.


## What this dashboard delivers

This dashboard provides a 360° view of Amazon sales performance by combining revenue trends, customer behavior, and product analytics into a single visually rich reporting platform.

It enables users to:

Monitor sales growth and seasonality
Identify top-performing products and categories
Track customer engagement through reviews
Evaluate performance by time period and product type

## Key Features

Dashboard (Power BI)
### An interactive dashboard that highlights:

Year to Date (YTD) Sales

Quarter to Date (QTD) Sales

Total Units Sold

Customer Reviews

Monthly & Weekly Sales Trends

Category Performance

Top Products by Sales and Reviews

Interactive Filter Controls (Product & Quarter)

# Business Value

This dashboard empowers Amazon sellers and analysts to:

Detect sales trends early
Improve product mix and pricing strategies
Increase customer satisfaction using review insights
Make data-driven marketing and inventory decisions
